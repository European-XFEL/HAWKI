Welcome to the guide for handling RAY (XFEL implementation of HAWKI platform), which provides language models from OpenAI and open-source models via XFEL.

This guide is intended to provide all staff members with a brief guideline on how to responsibly handle this technology. Please note that this guide supplements the terms of use of OpenAI and does not claim to be exhaustive. It is the responsibility of the users to comply with the terms of use.
